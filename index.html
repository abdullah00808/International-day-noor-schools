Welcome to International Day by Noor Language School

Discover the uniqueness of various countries, their culture, and their history.

Yemen

Yemen is known for its ancient culture, stunning architecture, and breathtaking landscapes. Visit the old city of Sana’a or explore Socotra Island.

Italy

Italy is the birthplace of the Renaissance and home to landmarks like the Colosseum, Venice canals, and Vatican City.

Mexico

Mexico offers vibrant traditions, stunning beaches, and rich history. Visit ancient Mayan ruins or colorful festivals like Día de los Muertos.

Japan

Japan blends ancient traditions with futuristic innovation. From cherry blossoms to the bustling streets of Tokyo, there’s something for everyone.

Germany

Germany is known for its castles, beer festivals, and rich history. Explore Berlin's vibrant city life or the scenic Bavarian Alps.

Morocco

Morocco is a land of vibrant souks, stunning desert landscapes, and architectural wonders like the Hassan II Mosque.

Turkey

Turkey bridges Europe and Asia, offering rich history, stunning mosques, and delicious cuisine like kebabs and baklava.

Al-Andalus

Al-Andalus, the historic Muslim Spain, was a center of knowledge, culture, and stunning architecture like the Alhambra Palace.
