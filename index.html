Welcome to International Day by Noor Language School

Discover the uniqueness of Yemen, Italy, Mexico, Spain, Japan, Germany, Morocco, Turkey, and Al-Andalus.

Yemen

Yemen is known for its ancient culture, stunning architecture, and breathtaking landscapes. Visit the old city of Sana’a, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, or explore the biodiversity of Socotra Island.

Italy

Italy is the birthplace of the Renaissance and home to magnificent landmarks like the Colosseum, Venice canals, and Vatican City. Enjoy the delicious cuisine, from pasta to pizza.

Mexico

Mexico offers vibrant traditions, stunning beaches, and rich history. Visit ancient Mayan ruins or enjoy colorful festivals like Día de los Muertos.

Spain

Spain is famous for its flamenco music, Mediterranean beaches, and historic sites like the Alhambra in Granada and Sagrada Família in Barcelona.

Japan

Japan blends ancient traditions with futuristic innovation. From cherry blossoms to the bustling streets of Tokyo, there’s something for everyone.

Germany

Germany is renowned for its engineering marvels, beer culture, and historical landmarks like the Berlin Wall and Neuschwanstein Castle.

Morocco

Morocco offers a rich blend of Arabic, Berber, and European influences. Explore the vibrant souks of Marrakech or the stunning Atlas Mountains.

Turkey

Turkey is a crossroads of cultures, featuring iconic sites like Hagia Sophia, Cappadocia, and the ancient city of Ephesus.

Al-Andalus

Al-Andalus represents the rich Islamic heritage in Spain, marked by architectural wonders like the Alhambra and cultural advancements during its golden age.
